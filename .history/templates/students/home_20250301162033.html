About Department

Welcome to the Department of BBA(Computer Application) at S.M. Joshi College, Hadapsar. Our department is designed to offers a unique blend of business and computer science education, equipping students with the skills to excel in today's technology-driven business landscape. Department of Bachelors of Business Administration in Computer Application was established in academic year 2006-2007. It is one of the most significant departments in the college. At present more than 500 students have enrolled for various courses offered by the department.
