S. M. Joshi, Hadapsar College

S. M. Joshi, Hadapsar College, a proud institution under the esteemed Rayat Shikshan Sanstha, Satara, is on a mission to impart education to the downtrodden masses. Established in 1919 by the visionary Late Padmabhushan Dr. Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil, our founder envisioned a realm of education that transcends social barriers and uplifts the deprived. Driven by the belief that education eradicates social evils, he established schools and colleges in neglected, inaccessible areas, shaping promising lives and instilling values of self-help and dignity of labor.
